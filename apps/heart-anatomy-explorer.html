⚠ Marco's Level (85% Blockage)
At 85% blockage, the LAD — nicknamed the "widow-maker" — delivers dangerously little blood to the anterior wall of the left ventricle. During exertion, the heart can't get enough oxygen, causing chest pain (angina). Without intervention, a complete blockage and massive heart attack are imminent.
🚨 Complete Blockage — Heart Attack (STEMI)
Total occlusion of the LAD causes an ST-Elevation Myocardial Infarction. The anterior wall of the left ventricle begins dying within minutes. Up to 40% of the heart muscle is at risk. Emergency PCI (angioplasty + stent) or thrombolytics are needed immediately. Mortality is high without rapid treatment.
